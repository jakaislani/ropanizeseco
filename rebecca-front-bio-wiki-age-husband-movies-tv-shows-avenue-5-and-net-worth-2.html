Rebecca Front Biography

Rebecca Front is a famous English actress, writer, and comedian. She is well known for her roles in various British comedies, including the Nighty Night (2004–2005), the sketch shows Big Train (2002), Knowing Me, Knowing You… with Alan Partridge (1994), Time Gentlemen Please (2000–2002), The Day Today (1994), and the radio show On The Hour (1992).

She also won the 2010 BAFTA TV Award for Best Female Comedy Performance for The Thick of It (2009–2012).
